GITAM Shore Fest 2026

GITAM Shore Fest is the most vibrant beachside cultural festival celebrating music, dance, sports, and creativity with the energy of students and the beauty of the sea.

Fest Highlights

  • Beach DJ Night
  • Live Music & Dance Shows
  • Sand Art & Photography
  • Food Stalls and Fun Games

Venue

The event will be held at the beautiful GITAM Beach Campus, Visakhapatnam, offering a perfect blend of celebration and sea breeze.
